Deliverer FAQ

How do I claim an order?

1. Navigate to the ‘Browse’ page.


2. Click ‘See Details’ for the order you’d like to claim.


3. After reviewing this order’s details to ensure that you are able and willing to purchase and deliver these items, click the ‘Claim Order’ button.



How do I make a delivery?

1. Follow the instructions under the ‘How do I claim an order?’ question to claim an order.

2. Under the ‘Deliveries’ tab, locate the delivery you’d like to make and click the ‘See Details’ button.


3. Purchase all of the items requested by the customer at the Princeton U-Store. If you cannot find an item, mark it as out of stock using the ‘Mark out of stock’ button under the item’s name.


4. On the details page, click the name of the customer to receive the customer’s phone number. Kindly let them know you are on your way!


5. When you arrive at the room and building specified in the delivery details page, knock and let the customer know that the order has been delivered.

6. Kindly remind them to confirm the delivery of their order in-app.


How do I view my current balance?

1. Navigate to the ‘Account’ page.


2. Your weekly (unpaid) balance is displayed under the ‘Balance’ heading.



How am I paid?

Deliverers are paid their balance in full weekly through Venmo. If you are not receiving payments properly or have a specific question, don’t hesitate to reach out to us at tigertask.princeton@gmail.com.
